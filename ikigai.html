Overview "Ikigai" introduces the Japanese philosophy of finding purpose and joy in life by aligning what you love, what you're good at, what the world needs, and what you can be paid for. It draws lessons from Okinawa's centenarians to promote longevity, happiness, and resilience while emphasizing mindfulness, gratitude, and flow in daily activities.
